About Our School

FBCS is a ministry of Faith Baptist Church Riverside. We offer preschool & primary education. Our goal is to prepare pupils to become critical thinkers, skillful workers, and good Christian citizens grounded in a biblical worldview.

Our Mission

To provide quality education with a biblical worldview, nurturing students to become critical thinkers, skillful workers, and good Christian citizens.

Our Vision

To be a leading Christian educational institution that equips students for academic excellence and spiritual growth, impacting our community and the world for Christ.
